Spring in Kyoto is truly something special. Taking a short break from debugging PyTorch code and analyzing chemical datasets, I went out to enjoy the *sakura* (cherry blossoms). 

The weather was perfect, and the blooming flowers transformed the streets and parks into a beautiful sea of pale pink. It is moments like these that remind me to balance intense academic research with the simple beauty of everyday life in Japan.
